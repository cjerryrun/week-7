Racquetball was created in the 1950's by Joseph Sobek. Racquetball is very similar to handball and British Squash.


Raquetballs History with the Olympics

So far racquetball has not been included in the Summer or Winter Olympics. Racquetball is a somewhat peculiar sport in that it is played indoors in an inclosed space. It does not identify as a seasonal sport. The International Racquetball Federation has put in 3 bids to participate in the Olympic Games. In 2016 squash was rejected from inclusion in the 2020 Japan games.
